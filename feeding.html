Feeding Ball Python

Snakes eat rats

Ball pythons can be picky due to their docile nature.

Some will only eat certain colors, or at a certain temperature. Some have no issues.

The best way to feed Ball Pythons is using Frozen Thawed rats. There is no chance that the rat will bite.

Rats bites have been known to cause infection.
